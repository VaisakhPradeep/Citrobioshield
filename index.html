Myths & Facts

MYTH 1

99.9% to 99.9999% . It's all the same.

FACT 1

Efficacy(kill rate) of a product is measured by its log reduction. 99.9 is 3 log reduction & 99.9999% is 6 log reduction.The term “log reduction” indicates a 10-fold reduction.

If there are one million bacteria present on a surface,
1 log reduction would reduce bacteria by 90%/ 100,000 bacteria remaining
2 log reduction removes 99 percent, leaving behind 10,000 bacteria
3-log removes 99.9 percent to leave behind 1,000 bacteria
6-log leaves behind only one cell in one million. With CBS you are shielded.

MYTH 2

Only Alcohol based sanitizers can kill germs

FACT 2

While it is proven that alcohol kills germs, there is also substantial evidence that bioflavonoid based sanitisers kill germs with better efficacy/kill rate.

MYTH 3

Odour/fragrance should be there in a sanitiser

FACT 3

Odour reduces the efficacy of the sanitiser. Odourless sanitiser ensures better germ killing rate.

MYTH 2

Products containing words like bio / eco are organic products.

FACT

Most products just contain one/ two organic ingredients and the rest of the composition contains chemicals. Please check the ingredients before you buy an organic product.
